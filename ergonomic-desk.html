The Science Behind An Ergonomic Standing Desk

Ergonomics is the study of how people work within their environments. A popular idea is that people can reduce the risk of injury and improve productivity levels when they use safer work practices. For those who sit at a desk on a regular basis, taking account of ergonomics might be changing the type of chair, type of desk, or behaviors demonstrated during a workday. Switching to ergonomic standing desks can reduce many health risks associated with office work.
